How To Play Piggy

  • Two players, each should fill the two boxes with their names.
  • Click the Proceed button.
  • Click the Roll button to play.
  • If any of your dices displays one(1) dot, you'll loose all your points but not your score.
  • Click any of the dices to roll again and boost your points.
  • Click the points to add them to your score.
  • The first person to get a score of 150 wins.
